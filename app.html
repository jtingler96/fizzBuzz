Fizz Buzz: A Coding Project

This app prints integers from 1 to 100, the integer will be replaced by Fizz if it is divisible by 3, Buzz if it is divisible by 5, and FizzBuzz if it is divisible by both 3 and 5.

Instructions

Enter a Fizz and Buzz input and press Submit. The results are then displayed in a table.
